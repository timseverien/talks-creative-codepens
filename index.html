  • Cellular Automaton called Conway’s “Game of Life”
  • A grid of cells
  • Each cell is either alive (black) or dead (white)
    1. Live cell with fewer than two or more than three neighbours dies (over/underpopulation)
    2. Live cell with 2 or 3 neighbours lives (healthy population)
    3. Dead cell with exactly three neighbours becomes a live cell (population for reproduction)
  • Specifying initial state yields a specific result
