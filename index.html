  • Computer Science
  • This is a Cellular Automaton
  • Conway’s “Game of Life”
  • Rules:
    1. Live cell with fewer than two or more than three neighbours dies
    2. Live cell with 2 or 3 neighbours lives
    3. Dead cell with exactly three neighbours becomes a live cell
  • By specifying initial state, you can make it do things
  • Game of Life is great
  • Harvard research
  • Lanes toward the center have more antibiotics, up to 1000x
  • Bacteria evolve to survive
